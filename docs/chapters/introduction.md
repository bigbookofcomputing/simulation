## What is Computational Science?

Computational science is an interdisciplinary field that uses advanced computing techniques to solve complex scientific and engineering problems. It combines elements of mathematics, computer science, and domain-specific knowledge to create simulations, models, and analyses that help us understand and predict natural phenomena.
